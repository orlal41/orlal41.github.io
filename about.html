What is Gmax Gym?

G Max Gym is a countryside gym so members can enjoy getting away from the busier towns and traffic is never a problem.
The gym is located in Whitehall ,Castlepollard, Co.Westmeath.
The gym is in operation since 2014 and has evolved in those years into a purpose built private gym facility offering a very supportive,fun and safe environment to the local community to help with their fitness needs.
Small group training is our main offer to new members.
This consists of groups of 4 to 8 people training together under the supervision of a trainer.
All sessions are structured and follow a training plan. Each exercise can be regressed or progressed depending on your current fitness/strength levels.
